20 Incredible Sights in Sitka, Alaska

Sitka National Historical Park (Totem Park)

Explore Tlingit and Haida totem poles lining a coastal trail and learn about the Battle of Sitka.

Alaska Raptor Center

Meet resident eagles, owls, hawks, and falcons at this rehabilitation center.

Fortress of the Bear

Observe rescued brown and black bears in large, naturalistic habitats.

St. Michael's Cathedral

Iconic, onion-domed Russian Orthodox cathedral filled with historic art and icons.

Sheldon Jackson Museum

Discover a diverse collection of Alaska Native artifacts and cultural items.

Sitka Sound Science Center

Explore marine life through touch tanks, aquariums, and a salmon hatchery.

Baranof Castle State Historic Site (Castle Hill)

Site of the Alaska purchase transfer, offering panoramic town and water views.

Mount Edgecumbe Trail

Challenging hike up a dormant volcano offering spectacular views (requires boat access).

Wildlife Viewing Tours

Take a boat tour for chances to see whales, sea otters, puffins, and more.

Russian Bishop's House

One of the few surviving examples of Russian colonial architecture in North America (part of NHP).

Sitka Historical Museum

Learn about Sitka's rich history, from Tlingit heritage to Russian and American periods.

Harbor Mountain Trail & Road

Drive or hike up for stunning alpine scenery and views over Sitka Sound (road seasonal).

Starrigavan Recreation Area

Features an estuary loop trail great for birding, picnic areas, and campground access.

Sitka Public Library

Modern building with great views, local exhibits, and free Wi-Fi.

Sitka Pioneer Home

Historic state-run retirement home with distinctive architecture, viewable from the outside.

Whale Park

A scenic viewpoint specifically designed for shore-based whale watching (seasonal).

Tongass National Forest

Explore the vast temperate rainforest surrounding Sitka via numerous trails and access points.

Indian River Trail

Relatively flat, accessible trail following the river through lush forest, leading to a waterfall.

Kayaking/Paddleboarding

Explore the calm waters, islands, and coastline of Sitka Sound up close via kayak or SUP.

Fishing Charters

Experience world-class salmon and halibut fishing with local guides.
